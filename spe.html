Student Performance in Exams

A dive into student performance and how other factors affect others

This was my first really big dive into exploration of data. Higher grades in other subjects correlated the best to other grades. Standard lunch was also a slight corrleation. my hypothesis was that parents education level allowed for the paying of standard lunch, but also that the parents education pushed the students to do better. what isnt represented here is how impactful free lunch is over not having it.

Although this data is fictious, i was able to see alot of similarities to real life. gender data, ethnicity were removed in an attempt to make the data, only about the data and not drawning concluions based around either topic.
